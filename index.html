Our story

About The Fisherman

Our family has a long history of fishing these waters. Our ancestors fished to feed their families; later our father made his living as a full-time fisherman. Together we opened a seafood restaurant in An Bang when it was still a sleepy seaside village.

Times have changed. Over-fishing and plastic pollution mean seafood is no longer healthy or sustainable. Together we decided to create An Bang's very first vegan restaurant by the sea.

Almost a decade on, we serve fresh, nutritious local produce in the beauty and tranquility of our garden — reflecting our respect and love for animals and the natural environment.

In honour of our forefathers we kept the name The Fisherman — the good fisherman who cares for the wellbeing of all living things. Enjoy your meal, prepared and served with love.

Frequently asked questions

Is everything on the menu vegan?
Yes — The Fisherman is 100% plant-based. We serve breakfast, bowls, curries, pizza, cocktails and fresh juices in our garden by An Bang Beach.
Do you take reservations?
Yes — use the reservation form on this page or message us on WhatsApp or Zalo.
What are your opening hours?
We are open daily from 08:00 to 21:00.
What is the price range?
Most dishes are 60,000–140,000 VND. Drinks and cocktails are listed separately on the menu.
Do you offer takeaway or pickup?
Yes — order online via WhatsApp, Zalo or email, or dine in our garden.
Where are you located?
You'll find us at An Bang Beach, Hội An Tây, Đà Nẵng, Vietnam — a garden setting steps from the sand.
Is the restaurant family-friendly?
Yes — we are family-friendly with a spacious garden setting. Dogs are welcome.
Do you have parking and Wi-Fi?
Free Wi-Fi is available (ask staff for the password). Street parking and scooter parking nearby.

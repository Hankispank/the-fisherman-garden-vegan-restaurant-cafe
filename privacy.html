Privacy policy

When you order or book a table we ask for your name, contact details, and order or reservation information.

Your submission is saved in our secure order log and you also send the details to us via WhatsApp or Zalo so we can confirm quickly.

We use this information only to prepare, confirm, and fulfil your order or booking. We do not sell your data.

Order records are kept for up to 200 recent submissions in our admin system, then rotated. To request deletion of your data, contact us by phone or message.
